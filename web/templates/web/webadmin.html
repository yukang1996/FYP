Pothole Detection and Classification System

Heavy rain storms and high congestion of traffic results in formation of potholes. Potholes results in safety hazards and transportation deficiency. To address this matter, we provide a platform for anyone to report pothole around University Malaya to the authority, Department of Development & Asset Maintenance UM.

Instruction

Please read carefully and follow the steps to complete the report.

Name: Please enter your Full Name.

Email: Please enter your Email address.

Phone Number: Please enter your Phone Number.

Location: Please enter the Location of pothole(eg. UM Library).
